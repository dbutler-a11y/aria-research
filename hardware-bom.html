Important Notes for ARIA Guide

  • Laser Safety: The 5mW 520nm laser is Class 3R. While safe for brief incidental exposure, avoid direct eye contact. The floor-facing orientation minimizes risk during normal use.
  • Walker Compatibility: Mounting bracket fits standard rollator frames (1" tubing). Verify your walker dimensions before purchasing.
  • Battery Life: Expect 4-6 hours of active use per charge depending on usage patterns.
  • Weather Resistance: Not rated for outdoor use in rain. Consider a protective cover for outdoor walking.
