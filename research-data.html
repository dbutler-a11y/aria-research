Clinical Evidence: Why This Works

68% Reduction in Freezing of Gait

Harvard SEAS studies demonstrated that rhythmic auditory cueing (metronome) reduces freezing episodes by 68%. Their soft robotic exosuit improved walking distance by 55%.

Source: Harvard SEAS, January 2024

Remote Monitoring Reduces Readmissions

Patients with telemedicine/RPM follow-up were 45% less likely to be readmitted within 30 days compared to those with no follow-up. RPM shows clear downward trends in hospital readmission risk.

Source: npj Digital Medicine, 2024

94% Response Rate to Visual Cueing

Laser line visual cueing showed immediate effect in 94% of Parkinson's patients experiencing freezing of gait. External cues activate bypass neural pathways around damaged basal ganglia.

Source: PMC/BME Frontiers, 2025

Satellite IoT: Global Reach for $5/month

New satellite IoT services now cost $5/device/month vs $50+ previously. 3GPP Release 17 enables existing cellular IoT devices to connect to satellites with software updates only.

Source: SpaceNews: Swarm Pricing, 2024
